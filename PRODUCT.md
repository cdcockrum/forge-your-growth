# Forge

> Understand who you're becoming.

---

# Mission

Forge helps people intentionally shape their lives by transforming daily actions into evidence-based understanding.

Most productivity apps track what you did.

Forge explains who you are becoming because of what you repeatedly do.

---

# Vision

Forge is a Personal Intelligence Platform.

It combines planning, reflection, evidence, visualization, and AI-assisted reasoning into a single operating system for lifelong growth.

Rather than optimizing tasks, Forge helps people understand the evolution of their identity.

---

# Product Principles

## Identity over Habits

People do not become writers because they complete writing tasks.

They become writers because evidence accumulates over time.

Forge reinforces identity rather than streaks.

---

## Evidence over Opinion

Every recommendation should be traceable to observable behavior.

No motivational clichés.

No arbitrary scores.

Every conclusion should have evidence.

---

## Story over Statistics

Humans understand stories better than dashboards.

Charts support the story.

They are not the story.

---

## Intelligence over Analytics

Analytics answer:

"What happened?"

Forge answers:

"What does it mean?"

---

## Calm over Clutter

Every screen should answer one question.

If multiple features compete for attention, simplify.

---

## Progressive Disclosure

Beginners see simplicity.

Experts can explore deeper layers of evidence.
